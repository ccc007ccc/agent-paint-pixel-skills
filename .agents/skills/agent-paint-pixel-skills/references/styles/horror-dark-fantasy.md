# Horror/Dark Fantasy

Use this style for monsters, caves, cursed items, ruins, demons, undead, and unsettling portraits.

## Visual DNA

- Large dark masses.
- Broken or irregular silhouettes.
- Selective cold highlights.
- Texture implying decay, slime, bone, stone, torn cloth, or corrosion.

## Palette

- Use 6-14 colors.
- Keep a dark ramp dominant.
- Use small cold highlights or sickly accent colors.

## Layer Order

Top to bottom:

```text
eyes_or_focal_detail
effects
cold_highlight
texture_scars
material_rot
base_mass
broken_outline
deep_shadow
```

## APX Guidance

- Use chunks for cracks, scars, eyes, slime, and torn edges.
- Keep most of the sprite in shadow.
- Use `clear_layer` or replacement chunks to remove accidental cute or clean details.

## Do

- Use asymmetry.
- Let darkness define form.
- Make one focal point readable.

## Avoid

- Even lighting.
- Too many bright colors.
- Symmetrical cute shapes unless intentionally subverted.
